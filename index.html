About Us

Technical supply for entrance automation and security access.

GPS Muscat is a local supplier focused on entrance automation, security access, and supporting hardware. We help customers find suitable products for gates, barriers, access control, locks, and automation accessories, with offline sales and direct communication for product inquiries.

Main categories we supply

Gate Automation

Sliding gate motors, swing gate kits, underground motors, control panels, limit switches, racks, and motor accessories.

Boom Barriers
